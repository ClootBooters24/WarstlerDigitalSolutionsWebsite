Privacy Policy

How we collect and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Warstler Digital Solutions ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and message content.
  • Email Communications: If you sign up for our newsletter or email updates, we collect your email address.
  • Service Inquiries: When you request information about our services, we may collect additional details about your business needs.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This includes IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These include session cookies and persistent cookies for analytics purposes.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, and browser version.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • To deliver the services you request
  • To improve our website and services based on user behavior analytics
  • To send you marketing communications (if you've opted in)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect against fraud and ensure security

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us (e.g., email service providers, hosting providers, analytics platforms)
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track website usage. Google may use this data subject to their own privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Contact form submissions are retained for 2 years from the date of submission. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You may request to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or contacting us directly.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your information in a portable format.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website (Google Analytics)
  • Performance Cookies: Improve website performance and user experience

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can choose to disable cookies, though this may affect your browsing experience.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing your personal information.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the revised policy on our website. Your continued use of our website constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
